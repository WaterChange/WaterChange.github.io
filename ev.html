Evaporation is..?

Information and to-the-root understanding.

Evaporation is when a liquid is hit and reached by heat and has transformed to a gaseous phase.

Evaporation is the process in which liquid is turned into vapor. When energy from the sun (heat) reaches water, the water goes into a gaseous state and rises from its area. The liquid becomes vapor due to the temperature emitted by the sun's heat. Less temperature in heat equals the slower water evaporates. So, when water is "boiled" by sun energy, the water becomes vapor and slowly rises to the clouds, where condensation happens...
