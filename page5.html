Evaporation:
1. Get 4 beakers (mL) - start for first trial
2. Fill beakers with water
3. Measure the water currently in the beakers in millimeters
4. Take temperature of the water
5. Record data
    6. Leave the beakers under a lamp for one day
7. Take temperature of the water again
8. Measure the water in the beakers in milliliters
9. Record data
         10. Repeat steps 1-5 for the second trial
