Research

For the time being, I have been researching water change/water forms. There are different forms of water in which it could be changed from a liquid state to a gaseous state or a liquid to solid, all according to temperature.

|Vocabuary and useful information to help make greater sense of the topic|

-Vapor is one form of water, which is produced during water evaporation.

-Evaporation is when water (or another liquid) is hit by heat and is changed into a gaseous phase.

-Another form of water is ice, which is basically frozen water. Due to a very cold temperature the water becomes a solid, ice.

-Temperature is the state of heat level of a substance or object whether decreasing or increasing.

-Alter means to change and, in this case, molecules are being altered because they are changing the space between them as they transform into another form.
