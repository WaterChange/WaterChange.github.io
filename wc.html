Other Changes in Water?

There are other changes in water, most coming from the water cycle.

Other changes of water include: Condensation and Precipitation. Ice is a form of water.
Condensation is the stage in which water vapor becomes liquid again, after evaporation. Condensation is most known to happen in clouds. After condensation is precipitation. Precipitation is when the liquid from condensation is converted to hail or snow, or stays the same as liquid. Rain is part of precipitation and after precipitation the whole process keeps going over and over again, IT'S A CYCLE!. Ice is the only solid formed from water. Ice forms when water is at it's freezing point (when it is at a really cold temperature). Ice melts due to heat, that is how water goes back to it's liquid state.
